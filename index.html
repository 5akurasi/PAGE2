Coffee Is Best Human Friend

How Coffee Work

Coffee affects the body through caffeine, a natural stimulant. When you drink coffee, caffeine quickly enters your bloodstream and reaches the brain, where it blocks adenosine—a compound responsible for making you feel tired. As a result:

1. Increased alertness and focus – Less fatigue, more energy.
2. Boosted metabolism – Coffee can slightly speed up metabolism.
3. Dopamine release – Improves mood and motivation.
4. Enhanced physical performance – Coffee increases adrenaline levels.

The effect lasts around 3–6 hours, depending on the person. After that, caffeine wears off, and fatigue may return.
